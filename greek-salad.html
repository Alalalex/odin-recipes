Greek Salad

Greek salad is avery delicious and health meal.

Fun fact: Traditional Greek salads do not contain lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Feta Cheese
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Red Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Vinegar

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes and cucumbers.
  2. Chop onion.
  3. Toss tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, onions, oil, and vinegar in a larg bowl.
  4. Place block of feta cheese on top and drizzle with olive oil.
