Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

Tom and Jerry. Luis and Clark. Simon and garfunkel. Peanut Butter and Jelly.

A classic lunch. Some people say that humans have been eating Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwiches since the rise of the Ancient Egyptian Empire. Other people don't.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter. Any kind of Peanut Butter you like.
  • Jelly. Any kind of Jelly you like works. (I recommend purple the purple).
  • Sliced Bread.

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter across a piece of bread.
  2. Spread jelly across another piece of bread.
  3. Push each slice of bread together so that peanut butter and jelly sides touch one another.
  4. Serve.
